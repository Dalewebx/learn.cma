Your Learning Journey
How the Academy works
A structured path from application to certification, designed around your life as a Catholic musician.
📋
Apply
Submit your application with your musical background and programme of interest. The Academy reviews and approves enrolments on a cohort basis.
🔑
Get Access
Once approved, you receive your login credentials and are enrolled in your first course. Your cohort deadline is set and your learning begins.
📖
Learn
Work through modules in order. Each module contains video lessons, audio examples, reading notes, and downloadable sheet music and PDFs.
✍
Assess
Complete a module assessment after each lesson. Questions are drawn randomly from a question bank — you must score 100% to advance.
🎓
Earn Certification
After completing all online modules, sit your final in-person examination. Pass it and receive your Catholic Music Academy certificate.
Programmes Offered
Six courses. One calling.
Complete courses sequentially. Each builds on the last, forming a complete education in Catholic sacred music.
