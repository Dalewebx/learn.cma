Your Learning Journey

How the Academy works

A structured path from application to certification, built around your studies in sacred music.

I
Apply
Submit your application with your musical background and programme of interest. The Academy reviews enrolments on a cohort basis.
II
Get Access
Once approved, you receive your login credentials and are enrolled in your chosen course. Your cohort deadline is set.
III
Learn
Work through modules in order. Each module contains video lessons, audio examples, lesson notes, and downloadable resources.
IV
Assess
Complete a module assessment after each lesson. Questions are drawn randomly from a question bank — meet the pass mark to advance.
V
Certify
After completing all online modules, sit your final in-person examination. Pass and receive your Catholic Music Academy certificate.

Current Programmes

Apply for the programme that matches your interests and experience level.

Course 01
Theory of Ancient Music
Trace the origins of music from ancient civilisations through medieval sacred traditions to the emergence of modern harmony.
3 Modules~4 hrs
Course 02
Liturgical Music Theology
The theological foundations of sacred music in Catholic worship, from the Council of Trent to Vatican II.
4 Modules~5 hrs
Course 03
Choral Leadership
Skills to lead, coordinate, and inspire a choir from rehearsal through to Sunday Mass performance.
5 Modules~6 hrs

A practical guide to the role: voice training, score reading, liturgical planning, and administration.
4 Modules~5 hrs

6 Modules~8 hrs

4 Modules~5 hrs
