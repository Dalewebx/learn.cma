Your Learning Journey

How it works

Six clear steps from application to certification. Structured, tracked, and supported every step of the way.

1
Apply
Submit your application with your musical background and programme choice. No entry exam required.
2
Get Approved
The Academy reviews your registration, verifies your application, and sets up your student account.
3
Access Your Courses
Receive your login credentials and gain immediate access to your personalised learning portal.
4
Study Your Modules
Watch video lessons, listen to audio demonstrations, complete readings, and download supporting materials.
5
Pass Assessments
Each module ends with an assessment. Pass to unlock the next. Questions are drawn randomly from a question bank.
6
Get Certified
Complete all online modules, sit your in-person final examination, and receive your Certificate of Competence or National Diploma in Music.
Why CMA Online

Built for how you actually learn

🌎
Learn Anywhere
Study from any location at any time. All you need is a phone or computer and an internet connection. No commute. No fixed schedule.
📊
Structured Learning
Modules unlock progressively as you complete each stage. Content is sequenced so each lesson builds on the last, the way serious music education should work.
📈
Track Your Progress
Every lesson completed and every assessment passed is recorded. Your dashboard shows exactly where you are and what comes next.
