Your Learning Journey

How it works

Six clear steps from application to certification. Structured, tracked, and supported every step of the way.

1
Apply
Submit your application with your musical background and programme choice. No entry exam required.
2
Get Approved
The Academy reviews your registration, verifies your payment, and sets up your student account.
3
Access Your Courses
Receive your login credentials and gain immediate access to your personalised learning portal.
4
Study Your Modules
Watch video lessons, listen to audio demonstrations, complete readings, and download supporting materials.
5
Pass Assessments
Each module ends with an assessment. Pass to unlock the next. Questions are drawn randomly from a question bank.
6
Get Certified
Complete all online modules, sit your in-person final examination, and receive your Certificate of Competence or National Diploma in Music.
Why CMA Online

Sacred music education,
built for where you are

Rooted in the Liturgy
Every module is built around the Catholic liturgical tradition — Gregorian chant, polyphony, choir direction, and the sacred calendar. This is not a generic music course adapted for the Church. It was designed for it.
A nationally recognised qualification
Approved by the Delta State Ministry of Higher Education to award the Certificate of Competence and National Diploma in Music, in affiliation with the Film and Broadcast Academy, Ozoro. Your credential is real and nationally recognised.
Designed for your phone
The portal is built mobile-first because that is how most students in Nigeria study. Your progress saves between sessions. Assessments, videos, and downloads all work on your handset — no computer needed.
