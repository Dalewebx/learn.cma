Dev Tools
Handover preparation and data management. Master admin only.
⚠️ These actions are irreversible. Use only when preparing to hand over the platform.
🧹 Clear All Test People Data

Wipes every student account, application, progress record, quiz attempt, enrolment, and login session. Courses, modules, and settings are untouched. Everyone who tested simply reapplies when the school opens.

Deletes: students · applications · enrolments · progress · quiz attempts · sessions
Keeps: courses · modules · content · cohort · settings · admin accounts
📚 Clear Course Content Only

Wipes all module reading content, quiz questions, video URLs, audio tracks, and downloads. Course titles and module structure are preserved — lecturers just fill in real content.

Clears: reading content · quiz questions · video URLs · audio tracks · downloads
Keeps: course titles · module titles · module order · all people data
☢️ Full Handover Reset

Clears all people data AND all course content in one shot. Platform is handed over with structure intact but completely empty. Type HANDOVER to confirm.

Deletes: everything except course structure, admin accounts, and settings
